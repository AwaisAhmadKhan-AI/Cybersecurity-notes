# Computer Types (Introduction)

## Overview

Computers are no longer just **laptops and phones**. They now exist in many everyday objects like:
- Smart fridges
- Doorbells
- TVs
- Home appliances

These are all examples of different **types of computers working in different roles**.

---

## Key Idea

A computer is any device that can:
- Process data
- Follow instructions
- Perform automated tasks

---

## Main Insight

Computers are now:
- **Visible** (laptops, phones)
- **Hidden** (smart devices inside everyday objects)

---

## Example: Smart Devices (IoT)

A smart fridge (like “NexusCool Fridge X17”) is an example of:
- A normal appliance + built-in computer
- Connected to the internet
- Able to send and receive data

---

## Why This Matters

Understanding computer types helps you:
- Know how systems are designed
- Identify security risks
- Understand real-world technology

---

## Learning Focus

By the end of this topic, you will be able to:
- Identify different computer types
- Understand their purpose
- See where they are used in real life

---

## Key Takeaway

Computers are everywhere — not just in PCs, but embedded into everyday objects, forming a connected digital world.
